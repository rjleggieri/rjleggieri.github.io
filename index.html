Multimodal researchCase Studies in ML

The “I’m fine” effect.

When the words say one thing and the voice says another.

I introduced the Alignment Discrepancy Score (ADS), an interpretable measure of the distance between text-derived sentiment and audio-derived prosody in a shared valence–arousal space.

DatasetDAIC-WOZ clinical interviews
Result≈0.62 AUC on elevated PHQ-8
PrincipleSurface signal, don’t hide it

PraxiCom

A voice-based simulated patient for practicing complex clinical communication.

Built with nursing faculty at UT Austin, PraxiCom is an affect-aware simulated patient system for rehearsing emotionally complex, patient-centered conversations. It combines live voice interaction, structured personas, faculty observation, and feedback.

  • Real-time voice simulation
  • Faculty-authored patient personas
  • Observable sessions and structured evaluation

Grounded visual QA,
